‚ùì Expert FAQ

How much water should I drink daily? ‚ñº
General guidelines suggest 2.5-3 liters for adults, but individual needs vary based on body weight, activity level, and climate. A good rule is to drink enough that your urine remains pale yellow throughout the day.
Does coffee and tea count toward my water goal? ‚ñº
Yes, despite the diuretic myth, moderate coffee and tea consumption contributes to hydration. The diuretic effect is mild and offset by the fluid content. However, plain water remains optimal for hydration.
Can I drink too much water? ‚ñº
While rare, water intoxication (hyponatremia) can occur when consuming excessive amounts rapidly. Stay within 0.8-1 liter per hour and listen to your body's thirst signals. The tracker helps maintain healthy intake levels.
When is the best time to drink water? ‚ñº
Spread intake throughout the day. Key times include: upon waking (rehydrate after sleep), 30 minutes before meals (aids digestion), during exercise, and before bed (moderate amount). Avoid large amounts immediately before intense exercise.
