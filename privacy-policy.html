Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Privacy-First Promise

All NexusUtility tools process data locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our servers. Your data stays on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

Unlike most online services, we deliberately designed our tools to require zero personal information:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No personal data transmitted to our servers
  • No cookies used for tracking
  • No analytics that identify individual users
  • No selling or sharing of any user data

How Our Tools Work

Our tools use modern web technologies to function entirely within your browser:

  • localStorage: Some tools save your preferences or history locally on your device only
  • Client-side processing: All calculations happen in your browser, not on our servers
  • No database: We don't have a user database because we don't collect user data

You can clear all stored data anytime by clearing your browser's local storage or cache.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history. You can control ad personalization through Google's Ad Settings.

Ad serving is handled entirely by Google. We don't have access to any advertising-related data about individual users.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • CDN libraries: For loading JavaScript libraries (like QR code generation)

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data according to their terms.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to delete or export. However, you can:

  • Clear localStorage data through your browser settings
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode
  • Disable JavaScript (though tools may not function)
  • Block third-party cookies

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
