üìã Understanding Percentages

Percentages are one of the most practical mathematical concepts in everyday life. From calculating discounts while shopping to understanding interest rates on loans, percentages help us make informed financial decisions.

The word "percent" comes from the Latin "per centum," meaning "by the hundred." A percentage is simply a fraction with 100 as the denominator. For example, 50% means 50/100 or 1/2.

What's the difference between percentage and percentage points? ‚ñº
Percentage is a ratio (50% of 100 = 50). Percentage points measure absolute difference between percentages. If interest rises from 5% to 7%, that's a 2 percentage point increase, but a 40% relative increase.
